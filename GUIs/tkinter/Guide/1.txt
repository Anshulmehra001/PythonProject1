Call of Duty: Ghosts is a 2013 first-person shooter game developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activision.It is the tenth major installment in the Call of Duty series and the sixth developed by Infinity Ward.It was released for PlayStation 3, Wii U, Windows, and Xbox 360, on November 5, 2013,and served as the final entry for Nintendo platforms.The game was released with the launch of the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One.
